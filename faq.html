FAQ
What's happening?
Kristen and JJ are getting married! The wedding will be April 30th, 2026. The ceremony will be at San Francisco City Hall followed by an early evening reception at Stable Café. All guests are welcome to the ceremony and reception.
Can I bring my child?
Yes, children are welcome if it would be fun for you to bring them. Please let us know if you plan to bring a child when you RSVP.
What should I wear?
The dress code is cocktail.
Where should I stay?
We recommend not staying in the city center. Neighborhoods like the Haight, Japantown, Nob Hill, and the Mission are nice. Some options: Hotel Kabuki in Japantown, The Metro Hotel in Lower Haight, Petite Auberge in Nob Hill.
If you have any questions at all about where to stay don't hesitate to reach out to Kristen or JJ.
Where are Kristen and JJ staying?
Kristen and JJ are staying at Nob Hill Inn.
What will the weather be like?
The weather in San Francisco is typically mild but variable, bring layers. The historic average for April 30th is between 50°F and 63°F.
Will they be there?
Our cats, Byrne and Eno, will not be able to attend the wedding.
How do I get to the ceremony?
The ceremony is in the 4th Floor North Gallery of San Francisco City Hall.

Enter the building on the Polk Street side (also known as Dr Carlton B Goodlett Place). Once you get past security, walk a few steps, and you'll see the elevators on your left-hand side. Take the elevator to the 4th floor. The North Gallery is on the far side — exit the elevator and walk straight ahead past the South Gallery (which is behind you as you exit).
How do I get from the ceremony to the reception?
We will be providing Uber vouchers to get you from City Hall to Stable Café. Vouchers will be emailed to you using the email address you submitted with your RSVP.
What about parking?
We recommend taking public transit or Uber, but if you do drive, street parking can be difficult near both locations (though easier near Stable Café). For garage parking near City Hall, we recommend the Performing Arts Garage (360 Grove St), about a 6 minute walk. Near Stable Café, there is the Mission Bartlett Garage, about a 17 minute walk.
